ALUMNI NETWORKING HUB

Overview

The Alumni Networking Hub is a web-based platform designed to connect current students with alumni. It serves as a central point for communication, mentorship, and career opportunities. The platform aims to build a strong community by facilitating knowledge sharing and professional development among its members. It includes features such as a member directory, a job board, and discussion forums.
